### Common Questions

If you're experiencing issues with the app, try these steps first:

1. **App not loading data?** Check your internet connection and try again.
2. **Data looks incorrect?** Market data may be delayed. Pull to refresh for the latest prices.
3. **App crashing?** Make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store.

For all other inquiries, feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn.
